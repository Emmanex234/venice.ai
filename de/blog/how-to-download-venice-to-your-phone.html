How to download Venice to your phone

Venice.ai

Venice is a new AI chatbot powered by the leading open-source AI models, such as Nous Llama3 and Playground 2.5.

Venice enables anyone, anywhere, to access private and censorship-free generative AI.  

Here’s how to download it to your smartphone or tablet.

How do I install Venice on my phone / tablet? 

Apple iOS

To install Venice on iOS, you'll need to use Safari. Open Venice.ai in Safari on your iOS device:  

Tap the “Share” icon in Safari:

Select “Add to Home Screen” from the options:

Confirm the installation by tapping the “Add” button.

The Venice app will now appear on your mobile device home screen:

Android (without pop-up install prompt)

Open Venice.ai in Google Chrome on your Android device.

Tap the Install button. You'll be presented with another install prompt window. Tap Install again.

Tap "Add to home screen":

Venice will now appear on your Android home screen:

If you initially dismissed the prompt, you can still download Venice to your mobile device following the steps below:

Apple iOS

You must use the Safari browser to add Venice to your Home Screen.  

  1. Open Venice.ai in Safari on your iOS device.
  2. Tap the “Share” icon in Safari.
  3. Select “Add to Home Screen” from the options.
  4. Confirm the installation by tapping the “Add” button.

Android

  1. Open Chrome, go to Venice.ai
  2. Tap settings (three dots), scroll down, select “Install app”
  3. Tap “Add to homescreen”

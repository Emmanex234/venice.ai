What to consider when looking for an AI image generator

When picking a free AI image generator, a few key factors can make or break your experience. Here's what to keep in mind as you explore your options:

How accurate is the AI model?

The model's accuracy determines how well it translates your prompts into images. Better models can handle complex descriptions and style references, producing results that match your vision more closely.

Look for generators that consistently generate high-quality, detailed images aligned with the prompts you feed them. 

How fast can the AI generate images?

Speed matters when you're in a creative flow. Faster models let you iterate quickly, testing out different ideas without getting stuck waiting for each generation.

Think about how much rapid experimentation matters in your process. Some generators prioritize speed for quick iterations, while others might take longer but deliver higher quality results.

How private and uncensored is the AI model?

Privacy and creative freedom can vary wildly between platforms. Some store every prompt and image you create, while others might put strict limits on what kind of content you can generate.

Consider how much control you want over your data and artistic expression. If you're working on sensitive projects or just value your privacy, look for platforms that keep your creations to yourself.

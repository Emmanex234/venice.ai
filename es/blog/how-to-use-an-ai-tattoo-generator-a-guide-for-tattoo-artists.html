Pro tips for getting the best results from your AI tattoo image generations

Venice offers comprehensive customization options to achieve your desired results. The platform has over 70 distinct image styles to choose from, enabling you to create everything from realistic photos to artistic interpretations.

To access these advanced settings, click the menu icon in the top right corner:

The settings menu will appear.

Here, you can access options that give you precise controls over your designs:

Negative Prompts

Control what you don't want in your tattoo designs by specifying elements to exclude. This helps ensure the AI tattoo generator avoids unwanted characteristics or styles in your final output.

Design Adherence

Adjust how strictly the AI follows your design description. Higher values ensure the generator stays true to your specific tattoo design elements, while lower values allow for more artistic interpretation.

High-Resolution Output

Generate detailed, high-resolution tattoo designs that clearly show intricate linework and shading. This feature is essential for creating stencil-ready designs and showing clients exactly how their tattoo will look.

Seed Control

Using the same seed number helps maintain consistency across related pieces. This is invaluable when creating matching tattoos or developing variations of the same design for a client.

Generation Steps

Control how thoroughly the AI processes your design. More steps (25-30) can produce cleaner lines and more detailed shading, crucial for complex tattoo designs.
